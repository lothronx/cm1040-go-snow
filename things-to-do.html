ATTRACTIONS

Ice Sculptures

At GoSnow, we pride ourselves on our state-of-the-art gallery of ice sculptures. Not only do the beautiful pieces on display leave you speechless, but the unique layout of the artwork is what makes this feature so unique and special.

Ice Rink

Undoubtedly our most enjoyed feature from all age groups, our ice rink is one that can be skated on for hours. The rink allows you the perfect balance of time to practice your moves and then put them to the test in 'disco' mode.

Sledging car

The most fun thing to do is to slide around on our sledging cars! Always thoroughly enjoyed by all of our guests, so make sure you book your tickets in advance to avoid disappointment!
